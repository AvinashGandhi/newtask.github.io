About Us

Buddy Job is a young and quickly growing online employment solution connecting people who are seeking jobs with employers who are in need of great talent. We aim to become a global provider and provide a complete package of job search, career management, recruitment and other related services.

Innovation, ease, accessibility, and affordability is at the heart of our success and growth.

- 

We are improving the way people can apply for jobs.

- 

We are helping recruiters in hiring the best fit for their organization by matching the candidate profile with their requirement to the closest possible extreme.

- 

We are working hard towards bringing all job opportunities and candidates to one common platform, thereby acting as a bridge between them, to help them connect seamlessly.

Buddy Job is literally your job buddy as it helps people find the right jobs and helps companies find the right people.

Why Choose Us?

If you are a job seeker, you can get access to hundreds and thousands of job opportunities across industries, experience levels, and geographies. As you complete filling up your profile on Buddy Job, our team continues to search for relevant jobs for you. In just a click, your application reaches multiple recruiters in one go, saving your time and effort towards filling up multiple applications for the same position in different companies.

If you are a recruiter, we will do the initial rounds of screening for you and narrow down your search funnel, so you get only those candidate profiles that are an exact match to your requirement. This not only saves your time of screening irrelevant profiles, but also helps you hire the right talent in way less costs.
